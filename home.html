KISS KISS Photo Series

By Dutch filmmaker and photographer
Mees Peijenburg

This series focuses on why a kiss is not just a kiss, but a sensual touch that is often triggered by a positive interaction between two or more humans. KISS KISS displays different moments that take place after two lips touch; pleasure, giddiness, nervous excitement. The rawness & transparency of Mees’ series almost helps you form bonds with the kisses without making you feel uncomfortable.

F-OICE-S Commitment Statement

F-oice-s is an independent online publication that aims to provide a feminist lens to critical readings of our contextual setting; deconstructing what feminism means today. Our zine is committed to ensuring that our online space is a platform for everyone. However, we want to primarily centre and present a multivocal range of marginalised genders, LGBTQIA+ and BIPoC.

Zero tolerance police: F-oice-s does not condone nor tolerate any type of interactions on this platform that contribute to hate speech, racial prejudice, racial stereotyping, and degrading language.
