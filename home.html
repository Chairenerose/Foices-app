Sociopolitical

F-OICE-S Commitment Statement

F-oice-s aims to provide a feminist lens to critical readings of our contextual setting, deconstructing what feminism means today. We are committed to ensuring that our online space is a platform for everyone. However, we want to primarily centre previously marginalized voices by showcasing and presenting a multivocal range of WOC and non-binary p.o.c.

Zero tolerance police: F-oice-s does not condone nor tolerate any type of (virtual) interactions on this platform that contribute to hate speech, racial prejudice, and degrading language.
