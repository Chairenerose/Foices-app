Moluccan Activist Organisations

  • Change The Future of Maluku : A movement to raise awareness for equality, justice, dignity and freedom for the people of Maluku.
  • Entrepreneur network Maluku Amsterdam : A network committed to all generations of the Moluccan community in Amsterdam. They do this by organizing various (online) activities with the aim of strengthening the sense of community.
  • Association Ana Upu : Organises activities for the youth, adults and the elderly to maintain and improve the social cohesion of the Moluccan community in the municipality of Ede.
  • Building the Baileo : Organization for the Indigenous Peoples Of the Moluccan Islands.
  • Saka Mese Maluku : Independent Media Network for Maluku.
  • Maluku Docuseries by BAMM : Documentary Series about Maluku; the land, sea and its people.
  • Petition for Njonja Soumokil : Petition to help Njonja Soumokil Investigate into the exact resting place of Mr. Dr. Ch. Soumokil
