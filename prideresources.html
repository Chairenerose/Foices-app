LGBTQIA+ Resources in the Netherlands

  • COC Amsterdam : Local association that strives for the decriminalization of sexual orientation and gender identity and for equal rights.
  • Love Exiles : Supports LGBT couples who have chosen or are considering exile in order to be together.
  • Amsterdam Student Association Gay : A Dutch student association for lesbian, homosexual, bisexual, transgender and queer students.
  • Maruf : Platform for queer Muslims.
  • Mama Cash : An international fund that supports women’s, girl’s, trans and intersex people’s movements around the world.
  • Black Queer Trans Resistance : BQTRNL is a Black LGBTI+ policital action group in the Netherlands.
  • Black Pride NL : Black Pride NL is a grassroots organisation and social justice movement centering and celebrating the Black LGBTQIA+ community and those of colour.
  • Pon di Pride : BPoC LGBTQ+ Dancehall/Caribbean party
  • De Stichting - Pride Amsterdam : Is a non-profit organization committed to emancipation, social, legal and social equality and acceptance of gay men, bisexuals and lesbians, and also for those who do not want to socially dominant gender roles or to conform.
  • Trans United : Trans United Europe/ Trans European BPOC network has been founded to unite the networks of European operating Trans BPOC NGOs and individual BiPOC trans activists living and working in Europe.
  • Bi-Cultural Transgenders : Founded by Ana Paula Lima, an organization for bi-cultural transgender people in the Netherlands.
